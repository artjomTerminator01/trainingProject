HOW THESE CALORIES ARE CALCULATED?

Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:

For men:

(10 * weight(kg) + 6.25 * height(cm) - 5 * age + 5) multiplier

For women:

(10 * weight(kg) + 6.25 * height(cm) - 5 * age - 161) multiplier

0-3 activity level - 1 multiplier

4-7 activity level - 1.15 multiplier

8-10 activity level - 1.3 multiplier

Carbs: calorie-intake * 0.5 / 4

Proteins: calorie-intake * 0.3 / 4

Fats: calorie-intake * 0.2 / 9
